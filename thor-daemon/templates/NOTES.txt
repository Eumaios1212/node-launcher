- Get the THORChain Node IP by running these commands:

  export NODE_IP=$(kubectl get pods --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} -l "app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "thor-daemon.name" . }},app.kubernetes.io/instance={{ .Release.Name }}" -o jsonpath="{.items[0].status.podIP}")
  echo $NODE_IP

- Visit RPC endpoint of the THORChain Node:

  Mainnet:
  curl http://$NODE_IP:27147

  Testnet / Mocknet:
  curl http://$NODE_IP:26567
